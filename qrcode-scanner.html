How it works

1

Upload your QR Code or scan it

Start by picking an image containing a QR Code or scan it using your webcam.

If the camera does not start, you need to follow the instructions below:

  1. Make sure you have allowed camera permissions.
  2. Click the "Start QR Code scan" button to start the camera manually.
2

Decoding your QR Code

FliQR decode your QR code instantly and securely with our browser-based service! No file is uploaded, processed nor stored on our server.

Utilizing advanced client-side processing technologies, our service analyzes the QR code image, locates its unique patterns, and extracts the encoded data.

3

Retrieve the decoded text

After decoding the QR code, you will see the results in the raw result text box.

So, you can easily copy your desired text, such as WiFi passwords, email addresses, or raw text

What is FliQR ?

FliQR is a free file-sharing service that offers a unique and convenient way to share files for free.
Our platform is user-friendly and straightforward. Simply start by uploading your desired file, and within seconds, FliQR generates a QR code that serves as a key to access the file.

This QR code can be easily shared with others, providing them with a direct download link to the file. Whether you're sharing documents, images, videos, or any other type of file, FliQR ensures a seamless and secure process. Our service eliminates the need for lengthy URLs or cumbersome email attachments, making file sharing a breeze.
